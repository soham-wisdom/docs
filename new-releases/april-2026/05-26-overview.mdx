The **May 2026** releases focus on keeping your domain accurate and up to date without adding manual overhead. This month, we are introducing **Continuous Context Extraction** to connect your domain directly to the live systems where your business logic lives, and **Knowledge Signals from Chat** to let you promote institutional knowledge directly from chat conversations into your domain knowledge base. Together, these updates ensure that your domain learns continuously from both your authoritative sources and your users' real interactions with data.
