## 🔍 Data Engineering & Knowledge

### **Context Extraction**
Context Extraction transforms your existing documentation—including **dbt YAML, LookML, PDFs, MCPs, and query logs**—into high-quality, standardized descriptions, metrics, and joins for your domain. 

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ingest Documentation">
    Connect your existing logs and YAML files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Generation">
    WisdomAI suggests descriptions and relationships.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review-First Workflow">
    Review and approve suggestions to maintain high-quality data standards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Knowledge Playground**
Before rolling out new experiences to the whole team, use the **Knowledge Playground**. It provides a dedicated place to explore, iterate, and test knowledge-driven responses to ensure they meet your accuracy standards.

## 📊 Dashboards & Visualization

### **Chat with a Dashboard**
You can now chat with a dashboard to ask questions about exactly what you are seeing. Responses are grounded in the specific dashboard context, allowing for deeper exploration of visualized data.

### **Visualization Enhancements**
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Dual Axis Charts" icon="chart-line">
    Combo charts let you visualize multiple series and chart types together (e.g., bar and line charts) for clearer comparisons and trend analysis.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Web Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Provide users the ability to ground WisdomAI responses with powerful web-based searches to augment internal data with external context.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 🔗 Integrations & MCP Support

### **Ask Data Questions in Slack**
**Slack in Channels** enables Slack-connected workflows directly from your channels. This keeps teams in their flow while collaborating, allowing for data-driven decisions without switching apps.

### **Model Context Protocol (MCP) Updates**
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="MCP in Dashboards">
    We’ve added support for MCP inside dashboards. Connect MCP-powered tools directly where your team works to run actions and surface results without leaving the dashboard experience.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="MCP Reviewed Queries">
    This feature extends reviewed queries to support visualizations generated from MCP tool calls. Users can **Thumbs-Up (👍)** MCP-generated visualizations to create examples that improve future responses.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
