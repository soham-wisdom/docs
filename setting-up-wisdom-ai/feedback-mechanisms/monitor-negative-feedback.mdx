---
title: "Monitor Negative Feedback"
description: "Learn to monitor the quality of responses using the Has Negative Feedback switch."
---

Administrators are responsible for reviewing and acting upon the negative feedback provided by all users. WisdomAI provides different options for you:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter Feedback" icon="check" titleSize="h2">
    View a list of responses with negative feedback only by navigating to the **Chat Log** tab within the WisdomAI interface and switching the **Has Negative Feedback** toggle.

    <img
      src="/images/negative-feedback-chat-log-v2.png"
      alt="Negative Feedback Chat Log Pn"
      title="Negative Feedback Chat Log Pn"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email Notifications" icon="check" titleSize="h2">
    The system automatically channels negative feedback to administrators via email, ensuring prompt attention to issues that require intervention.

    <img
      src="/images/feedback-email-for-admins.png"
      alt="Feedback Email For Admins Pn"
      title="Feedback Email For Admins Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />

    <Info>
      This is a Beta feature, reach out support at [support@askwisdom.ai](mailto:support@askwisdom.ai) to sign up for email notifications when negative feedback is submitted.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prioritization for Improvement" icon="check" titleSize="h2">
    Questions marked with negative feedback are automatically prioritized for knowledge base improvement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  For Domains with high usage or complex data, it is highly recommended to assign a dedicated administrator to regularly review feedback and make necessary updates to the knowledge base, ensuring continuous optimal performance.
</Note>
