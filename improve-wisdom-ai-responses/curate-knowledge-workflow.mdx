## View and Navigate Chat History

The **Chat Log** interface provides robust functionalities for viewing and tracking the history of your team's interactions, contributing to transparency and content reliability. The system maintains a comprehensive, chronological record of all communications.

1. Access the Chat Log, located in the left sidebar to open the main interface for reviewing past interactions.
2. View Chat Log entries. The Chat Log displays a tabular view of interactions, with each row representing a chat entry and providing key metadata:

- **Last Message Timestamp:** Clearly indicates the date and time of the most recent message within the chat, establishing a precise chronological order.
- **User Attribution:** Identifies the initiating or contributing user for each chat, ensuring clear accountability.
- **Domain Categorization:** Specifies the associated domain or category (e.g., "B2B Sales - 1Q25," "Customers"), facilitating organized access to information.
- **Chat Summary:** Offers a concise overview or the initial query of the interaction.
- **Response Count:** Displays the number of responses within a chat, indicating the depth of the conversation. Accompanying icons (e.g., üí¨ 1 ‚¨ÜÔ∏è 1) provide additional context on interaction types.

3. Filter and locate interactions. Users can efficiently locate specific historical interactions using various filtering options to streamline their review process:

- **Date Range Filtering:** Utilize "Start Date" and "End Date" fields to constrain the view to a specific timeframe, allowing for targeted historical review.
- **User-Specific Filtering:** Filter by user to display only chats associated with a particular team member, enabling focused review of individual contributions.
- **Domain-Specific Filtering:** Restrict entries to a selected domain or topic, facilitating the review of domain-specific discussions.
- **Keyword Search:** Employ the "Filter by summary" field to search for specific keywords or phrases within chat summaries, enhancing discoverability.
- **Filter Reset:** The "Clear Filters" button allows for quick removal of all active filters, restoring the full log view.

4. Access the detailed chat history to obtain a complete understanding of any interaction's progression and context by clicking the **View details** button corresponding to any chat entry opens a dedicated view or modal containing the full chat transcript. Here, you can:

- **Review Full Conversations:** The detailed view presents the entire sequence of messages, queries, and responses chronologically. This comprehensive record illustrates the evolution of information and discussions, providing complete context.
- **Identify User Contributions:** Within the detailed view, individual user contributions are clearly demarcated, aiding in accountability and tracing the origin of specific information.

### Leverage Chat History for Transparency and Reliability

The above-mentioned functionalities of the Chat Log contribute significantly to the integrity and trustworthiness of the knowledge base:

- **Comprehensive Audit Trail:** The system provides a complete audit trail, documenting all communications. This is invaluable for compliance, troubleshooting, and verifying information accuracy.
- **Enhanced Contextual Understanding:** Access to full chat histories provides critical context around decisions and shared information, mitigating misinterpretations and ensuring clarity.
- **Promotes Accountability:** Clear user attribution fosters accountability among team members, encouraging precise and reliable contributions as interactions are permanently recorded.
- **Ensures Immutable Record:** The system maintains an unedited historical record of all interactions. Any corrections or updates are appended as new messages, preserving the original context and enhancing the reliability of historical data.
