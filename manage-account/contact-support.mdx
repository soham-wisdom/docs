---
title: "Contact Support"
---

Whether you require technical assistance or have questions regarding any WisdomAI features, contact our support team via email at [**support@askwisdom.ai**](mailto:support@askwisdom.ai).

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:

- A clear description of the problem.
- Relevant screenshots or a short video of the issue.
- Any error messages or logs from previous runs.

We are here to help you get the most out of WisdomAI.
